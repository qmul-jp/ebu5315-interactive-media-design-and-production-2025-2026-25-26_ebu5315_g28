Circle Theorem 5: Radius to a Tangent

How to Explore

  • Drag the tangent point P around the circle.
  • The tangent line will rotate with point P automatically.
  • Observe: The radius is always perpendicular to the tangent at the point of contact.
  • The angle between radius and tangent is always 90°.
Angle between Radius and Tangent
90°
